Privacy Notice

Last updated: July 2026

QQMouse is designed to keep your data on your own devices. This notice explains what does and does not happen with your information.

The app

  • Runs locally. QQMouse forwards input, clipboard, and files only over your local network, directly between the devices you pair.
  • No accounts. There is no sign-up and no login.
  • No telemetry. It does not send your keystrokes, mouse activity, clipboard contents, files, or usage analytics to us or to any third party.
  • Pairing keys and settings are stored locally on your device, encrypted at rest, and never leave it except as part of the direct connection to a device you paired.

This website

  • Hosting. The site is served by Cloudflare Pages, which may process standard request data (such as your IP address and request logs) to deliver and secure the site. See Cloudflare's privacy policy.
  • Downloads. Installers are served from GitHub Releases; downloading is subject to GitHub's privacy statement.
  • No analytics or advertising cookies. We do not add trackers, analytics, or advertising to this site.
  • Local storage. The site stores a single value in your browser's local storage — your light/dark/system theme choice (qqmouse-theme). It stays on your device and is not sent anywhere.

Your choices

You can clear the theme preference at any time by clearing this site's data in your browser. Because the app collects nothing about you, there is no account or personal data for us to access, export, or delete.

Changes

We may update this notice as the software evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new date.
